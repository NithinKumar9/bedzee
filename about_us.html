About Bedzee

Bedzee is a modern, technology-driven marketplace transforming how people discover, book, and manage PG and hostel accommodations across India.

We bridge the gap between tenants searching for reliable stays and property owners seeking efficient management solutions. Our platform offers a seamless experience—from discovering verified properties to booking and moving in—ensuring convenience, transparency, and trust at every step.

FOR TENANTS

Simplify Your Accommodation Journey

Bedzee simplifies the entire accommodation journey. With a curated selection of verified PGs and hostels, users can easily search, compare, and book stays that match their preferences, location, and budget—without the usual uncertainty.

FOR PROPERTY OWNERS

Powerful Management Solutions

Bedzee provides a powerful all-in-one solution to list, manage, and grow your business. From property visibility and bookings to operational management, our platform is designed to streamline processes, improve occupancy rates, and enhance tenant satisfaction.

Our Vision

At Bedzee, we are driven by a vision to standardize and elevate the PG and hostel living experience across India. By combining smart technology with a customer-first approach, we aim to create a trusted ecosystem where finding and managing accommodations is simple, efficient, and reliable.
