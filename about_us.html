About Bedzee

Bedzee is a modern, technology-driven platform transforming the way people discover, book, and manage PG and hostel accommodations.

We connect property owners with tenants seeking safe, comfortable, and affordable living spaces, creating a seamless and reliable ecosystem built on trust and transparency.

Our platform is designed to simplify every step of the accommodation journey—from discovering verified properties to booking and moving in. By combining smart technology with a user-first approach, we ensure a smooth, convenient, and hassle-free experience for tenants.

At the same time, Bedzee empowers property owners and managers with efficient tools to list, manage, and grow their business. From increasing visibility to streamlining operations, we help owners maximize their property’s potential while maintaining high standards of service.

FOR TENANTS

Simplify Your Accommodation Journey

Bedzee simplifies the entire accommodation journey. With a curated selection of verified PGs and hostels, users can easily search, compare, and book stays that match their preferences, location, and budget—without the usual uncertainty.

FOR PROPERTY OWNERS

Powerful Management Solutions

Bedzee provides a powerful all-in-one solution to list, manage, and grow your business. From property visibility and bookings to operational management, our platform is designed to streamline processes, improve occupancy rates, and enhance tenant satisfaction.

Our Vision

At Bedzee, we aim to transform and standardize PG and hostel living by making quality accommodations more accessible through smart technology. We are building a trusted, transparent ecosystem where finding and managing stays is simple, reliable, and efficient-creating communities where tenants feel at home and property owners grow with confidence.

Bedzee is India's trusted platform to find and list PG's and Hostels. We connect tenants and property owners through trust, technology and transparency.
