Platform Usage Policy

Guidelines for safe, transparent, and efficient platform usage

Effective Date: [Insert Date] Last Updated: [Insert Date]

1 Purpose of This Policy

This policy establishes guidelines to ensure that all users experience a safe, transparent, and efficient platform.

2 Role of the Platform

Bedzee serves as:

  • A technology provider offering tools and insights to landlords
  • A discovery and connection platform for tenants

Our goal is to streamline the accommodation process while empowering users with better information and tools.

3 Responsibilities of Landlords

Landlords play a key role in maintaining platform quality. They are expected to:

  • Provide accurate, complete, and regularly updated property details
  • Ensure transparency in pricing, amenities, and policies
  • Manage the day-to-day functioning and tenant experience of their properties

4 Responsibilities of Tenants

Tenants are encouraged to:

  • Carefully review listings and property details
  • Communicate clearly with property owners
  • Make decisions aligned with their preferences and requirements

5 Listings and Content Integrity

To maintain trust:

  • All listings must be genuine and verifiable
  • Misleading or inaccurate content may be removed

Photos and media (including those captured during onboarding) may be used across:

  • Listings
  • Promotional campaigns
  • Platform branding initiatives

6 Marketing and Visibility

Properties listed on the platform may be promoted through:

  • Digital marketing channels
  • Social media campaigns
  • Other outreach initiatives

These efforts are designed to improve visibility and occupancy for landlords.

7 Training and Insights for Landlords

We may provide:

  • Market trends and industry insights
  • Suggestions to improve occupancy and pricing strategies

These inputs are intended to support better decision-making and growth.

8 Platform Integrity and Enforcement

To maintain a high-quality experience:

  • Listings or accounts that violate policies may be restricted
  • Appropriate action may be taken to ensure fairness and reliability

9 User Conduct

All users are expected to:

  • Act responsibly and respectfully
  • Use the platform in good faith
  • Contribute to a positive ecosystem

10 Independent Interaction Between Users

The platform enables connections, but interactions are driven by user intent.

Both landlords and tenants:

  • Engage with each other based on their own judgment
  • Are responsible for their agreements, actions, and outcomes

11 Governing Law

This policy is governed by Indian law. Jurisdiction: Bangalore
