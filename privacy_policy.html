1 Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bedzee ("Company", "we", "our", or "us"), a company incorporated under the laws of India in accordance with the Companies Act, 2013, collects, uses, processes, stores, and protects personal data of users ("you", "your", or "users").

This policy applies to:

By accessing or using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2 Nature of Our Services

Our platform is designed to bring efficiency and transparency to the accommodation ecosystem by operating as:

  • A Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform that helps landlords manage their properties, track expenses, handle bookings, and optimize pricing
  • A digital marketplace that enables tenants to discover, compare, and connect with PG/hostel accommodations

Important Clarification

Our role is to enable connections and provide technology support.

We do not directly handle:

  • Day-to-day property management
  • Maintenance or tenant services
  • Offline agreements or arrangements

These aspects are independently managed by landlords and tenants based on their mutual understanding and preferences.

3 Information We Collect

To provide a smooth and reliable experience, we collect different types of information:

3.1 Personal Information

When you register or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Address or location details
  • Profile photo (optional)

3.2 Account & Transaction Data

To enable platform functionality:

  • Booking details and history
  • Communication between tenants and landlords
  • Payment-related metadata (such as transaction status or reference IDs; we do not store sensitive card details)
  • Subscription details for landlord services

3.3 Property & Business Data (Landlords)

For listing and management purposes:

  • Property details (location, pricing, amenities)
  • Images and videos (including those captured during onboarding photography services)
  • Business-related inputs required for software tools

3.4 Device & Usage Data

To improve platform performance:

  • IP address
  • Device type, operating system
  • App usage behavior and logs
  • Cookies and tracking data

4 How We Collect Data

We collect information through various interactions, including when you:

  • Create an account or update your profile
  • List a property or search for accommodations

  • Communicate with other users through the platform

  • Engage with our marketing campaigns or promotions

We use your information to deliver, improve, and secure our services:

5.1 Platform Functionality

  • Creating and managing user accounts

  • Connecting tenants with landlords

5.2 SaaS Services (Landlords)

  • Providing property management tools
  • Tracking bookings and expenses
  • Offering analytics and pricing insights

5.3 Business Operations

  • Supporting property onboarding (including photography services)
  • Promoting listed properties through marketing initiatives
  • Providing training and advisory insights to landlords

5.4 Platform Improvement

  • Analyzing usage trends and performance
  • Enhancing user experience and platform features

5.5 Legal & Compliance

  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations
  • Preventing fraud and misuse
