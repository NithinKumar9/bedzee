Refund & Cancellation Policy

Clear guidelines for cancellations and refunds across our platform

Effective Date: May 3, 2026 Last Updated: May 3, 2026

1 Overview

This policy outlines how cancellations and refunds are handled across the Bedzee platform, ensuring clarity for both tenants and landlords.

2 Subscription Fees for Landlords

Subscription plans provide access to software tools and platform features that support property management.

  • These fees are generally non-refundable once the service is activated, as they provide immediate access to digital tools
  • Exceptions may be considered in cases such as duplicate transactions or verified technical issues

3 Booking-Related Payments

The platform facilitates discovery and connection, while the accommodation service itself is offered by individual landlords.

  • Each property may have its own cancellation and refund terms
  • Tenants are advised to review these terms carefully before confirming bookings

Important Note

This approach ensures flexibility for landlords while allowing tenants to make informed decisions.

4 Platform Fees

In some cases, platform/service fees may be charged for enabling bookings or transactions.

  • These fees are typically non-refundable once the service is successfully delivered
  • However, refunds may be processed in cases involving technical failures or unsuccessful transactions

5 Cancellations

By Tenants

Cancellations depend on the specific policies defined by the property owner.

By Landlords

Landlords are expected to communicate clearly and handle any applicable adjustments or refunds in a timely manner.

6 Refund Processing Timeline

Where refunds are applicable, they are processed within 5–10 business days, subject to the timelines of the respective payment providers.
